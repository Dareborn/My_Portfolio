Strategy Behind

I'm David Obafemi a media strategist and creative director with a track record of building and scaling brand presences across Nigeria's most competitive digital landscapes. Based in Ibadan, I work with brands of all sizes to craft content strategies that don't just look good they convert.

My work spans social media strategy, website design, AI-powered automation, content creation, and brand identity. Whether I'm directing a Reel, designing a landing page, or setting up an automated marketing workflow, I bring both creative instinct and strategic discipline to the table.

Every brand I've touched has gotten a tailor-made strategy no templates, no guesswork. From fashion academies and food brands to bridal boutiques and lifestyle communities, I understand how to position a brand and keep it growing.
